================================================================================
SECTION 1: SIGNAL EXTRACTION PROTOCOL
================================================================================

## THREE-LAYER EXTRACTION

Extract at three layers for each category:
- **LITERAL**: Explicitly stated (confidence 0.8-1.0)
- **INFERRED**: Implied by data (confidence 0.5-0.8)
- **ABSENT**: Expected but missing (flag as absent_signal)

## SIGNAL SCHEMA (required for ALL signals)

```json
{
  "signal_id": "SIG_001",
  "category": "strength|weakness|metric|anomaly|unused_capacity|avoidance|entity|cross_file",
  "layer": "literal|inferred|absent",
  "description": "string",
  "magnitude": 0.0-1.0,
  "actionability": 0.0-1.0,
  "impact_estimate": "dollar/time/resource estimate",
  "source_quote": "exact text from file",
  "reasoning": "one sentence classification logic",
  "data_quality": "hard_measured|soft_estimated|self_reported|inferred|stale",
  "relationships": [{"related_signal_id": "SIG_002", "type": "reinforcing|undermining|unexplained|dependent"}]
}
```

## EIGHT SIGNAL CATEGORIES

1. **ENTITIES**: People, teams, systems, products, customers, competitors
2. **METRICS**: KPIs, financials, performance data, counts
3. **STRENGTHS**: Positive capabilities, advantages, what works
4. **WEAKNESSES**: Problems, gaps, underperformance, risks
5. **ANOMALIES**: Unexpected patterns, outliers, contradictions
6. **UNUSED_CAPACITY**: Latent potential, underutilized resources
7. **AVOIDANCES**: Topics not addressed, elephants in room
8. **CROSS_FILE_PATTERNS**: Patterns emerging only from combining files

## ADDITIONAL OUTPUTS

**domain_context**: {industry, benchmarks_known, data_time_horizon, data_type: operational|strategic|personal|creative}

**temporal_dynamics**: [{metric_name, trajectory: improving|declining|stagnant|cyclical|inflecting, acceleration, inflection_points}]

**user_intent_inference**: {likely_question, decisions_facing, emotional_tone_of_data: anxious|confident|exploratory|urgent|defensive}

**importance_ranking**: Top 10 signals with rank and one-sentence reasoning

**contradiction_map**: [{signal_id_a, signal_id_b, type: within_file|cross_file|stated_vs_revealed|metric_divergence, description}]

## TRANSFORMATION MATRICES

| Matrix | Key Operators | Formula |
|--------|---------------|---------|
| Truth | W, Psi, Co, M | W * Psi * Co * (1-M) |
| Love | G, At, S, F | G * S * (1-At) * (1-F) |
| Power | P, R, E, I | P * I * (1-R) * E |
| Freedom | V, At, K, S | V * S * (1-At) * (1-K) |
| Creation | I, P, Co, R | I * P * Co * (1-R) |
| Time | P, K, A | P * A * (1-K) |
| Death | S, At, F, V | S * V * (1-At) * (1-F) |

## CASCADE CLEANLINESS

Levels: L7(>=0.9) L6(>=0.7) L5(>=0.5) L4(>=0.35) L3(>=0.2) L2(>=0.1) L1(<0.1)
Formula: avg(G,S,P,Co,W,A) - avg(At,R,F,M,K)

## BREAKTHROUGH DYNAMICS

breakthrough_prob = (purification * catalyst * grace_factor) / (1 + resistance)
- purification = (1-M) * (1-K) * Psi
- catalyst = G * Co
- resistance = At * R * F
- grace_factor = G^2 * S

Blockers: At>0.75, F>0.8, R>0.7

## BOTTLENECK THRESHOLDS

At>0.75, F>0.70, R>0.70, M>0.75, flow<0.25

## HIGH-IMPACT OPERATORS (feed multiple systems)

- G_grace: Love, Death, Breakthrough, Leverage (4)
- S_surrender: Love, Freedom, Death, Breakthrough (4)
- At_attachment: Love, Freedom, Death, all Bottlenecks (5+)

- No signals = lower confidence or null
