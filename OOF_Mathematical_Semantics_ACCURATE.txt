DERIVED OPERATOR VALUES (From OperatorEngine)
================================================================================

**klesha_total**
- Mathematical meaning: Total affliction load
- Calculated from: At + Av + Fe + De + Re (sum of afflictions)
- Higher = More afflicted state

**maya_effective**
- Mathematical meaning: Effective illusion strength
- Calculated from: M × (1 - W) (maya amplified by lack of witnessing)
- Higher = Stronger effective delusion

**consciousness_quality**
- Mathematical meaning: Overall consciousness quality
- Calculated from: Ψ × W × A (consciousness × witnessing × awareness)
- Higher = Better consciousness quality

**grace_availability**
- Mathematical meaning: Available grace magnitude
- Calculated from: G × (1 - K) (grace amplified by freedom from karma)
- Higher = More grace available

**karma_binding**
- Mathematical meaning: Degree of karmic binding
- Calculated from: K × (At + Hf) / 2 (karma amplified by attachment and habits)
- Higher = More bound by karma

**ego_separation**
- Mathematical meaning: Strength of ego boundary
- Calculated from operators
- Higher = Stronger ego separation

**resistance_total**
- Mathematical meaning: Total resistance force
- Calculated from: Re + Av (resistance plus aversion)
- Higher = More resistance to reality

**evolution_rate**
- Mathematical meaning: Speed of consciousness evolution
- Calculated from: As × (1 - Hf) × W (aspiration minus habits plus witnessing)
- Higher = Faster evolution

**love_fear_balance**
- Mathematical meaning: Balance between love and fear
- Calculated from: L - Fe (love minus fear)
- Range: -1.0 (pure fear) to +1.0 (pure love)
